## Content Format Requirements

All content must follow the SEO Content Strategy skill (`~/.claude/skills/seo-content-strategy/`) and AISO skill (`~/.claude/skills/aiso/`):

- **Answer-first format** — lead with a direct answer, then explain
- **Definition paragraph** — clear, AI-extractable 1-2 sentence definition per concept
- **TL;DR block** — key takeaways at top of every article
- **Question-format headings** — H2s as "How does X work?" style
- **PubMed citations** — numbered references with DOI links (YMYL required)
- **Comparison tables** — structured data for AI extraction
- **FAQ section** — 5-10 Q&A pairs per article
- **Internal linking** — 3-5 links per article (pillar, siblings, product)
- **Author byline** — "Reviewed by dermatologist/trichologist"
