## Unique NL Challenge: English Content Leakage

The NL page has a unique problem compared to DE and EN: significant untranslated English content in:
1. **H4 testimonial labels** (60 tags): "Easy to use", "New hair growth", "Thicker, healthier hair" — these appear to be from the testimonial section's Shopify metafield/content and were never translated
2. **H2 testimonial section heading**: "Visible hair growth within 2-4 months"
3. **H6 product subtitles** (26 tags): Product description HTML contains H6 headings

These are content issues (product data/section content), not theme template issues. Fixing requires editing product descriptions and testimonial section content in Shopify admin.
