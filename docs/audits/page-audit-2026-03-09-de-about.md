## Critical Finding — Thin Visible Content

The extreme model disagreement across 16 criteria (see below) strongly indicates that ChatGPT evaluated the visible HTML content while Gemini evaluated the structured data / schema markup. The About page almost certainly has very thin visible body text, with most information only present in schema. This is a structural content problem, not a copywriting problem. The fix requires adding substantial visible narrative content to the page — schema alone does not satisfy AI or human readers.

**Malcolm review required before any changes are made to visible content on this page.**

## Priority Fixes (sorted by impact)

1. Add substantial visible narrative content — founders story, mission, credentials, methodology; do not rely on schema alone
2. Add author/team information — named individuals with photos, roles, and credentials visible on the page
3. Add FAQ section — at minimum 5 Q&A pairs in `<details>/<summary>` format
4. Add recency signals — "Founded [year]", "Last updated [date]", recent milestone or news mention
5. Add internal links — link to Science page, product bestseller, and blog articles
6. Add multimedia — team photos, lab images, or brand video
