## AI Search Query Targets

These are conversational queries people ask AI assistants. Different from Google keywords — longer, question-format.

### 5 AI Question Categories

#### 1. Diagnosis Questions (Very High Volume)
- Why am I losing hair suddenly?
- Why is my hair thinning at the crown?
- How do I know if I have androgenetic alopecia?
- Is hair thinning reversible?
- What causes hair loss in men in their 30s?
- Why are my temples receding?
- Why is my hair shedding more than usual?

#### 2. Treatment Questions (Purchase Intent)
- What is the best treatment for hair loss?
- How can I regrow my hair naturally?
- Do hair growth serums actually work?
- Can hair grow back after thinning?
- What treatments stop hair loss?
- What treatment works better than minoxidil?
- How can I stop hair loss without finasteride?

#### 3. Microneedling Questions (Strategic Advantage)
- Does microneedling regrow hair?
- How deep should microneedling be for hair growth?
- How often should you microneedle the scalp?
- Does microneedling work without minoxidil?
- How long does microneedling take to regrow hair?

#### 4. Ingredient Questions
- What are copper peptides for hair growth?
- Does GHK-Cu regrow hair?
- Are peptides better than minoxidil?
- What ingredients stimulate hair follicles?

#### 5. Routine Questions (High Conversion)
- What is the best hair regrowth routine?
- How should I combine microneedling with serum?
- What weekly routine helps hair grow back?
- How long does it take to regrow hair?
- When does microneedling start working?

## Competitive Landscape

### Hims & Hers Health (Market Leader)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Monthly visits | ~6.6M |
| Referring domains | 12,000+ |
| Strategy | Educational content dominance — not product pages |
| Content types | Diagnosis → Treatment research → Comparison → Prescription purchase |
| Key strength | Full customer journey coverage |
| Weakness | Drug-focused (finasteride, minoxidil) — no mesotherapy/microneedling |

### Scandinavian Biolabs

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Strategy | Product science + brand narrative |
| Differentiator | Bio-Pilixin® proprietary formula |
| Claims | "97% less hair loss, 73% more density" |
| Funnel | Education → Routine → 3-step product system |
| Key strength | Routine-based selling (system, not single product) |
| Weakness | Limited content depth |

### The Ordinary (Ingredient SEO Model)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Strategy | Brand the molecule, not the product |
| Example | "niacinamide serum" > "The Ordinary serum" |
| Key lesson | Ingredient keywords convert extremely well |
| Applicable to Hairgenetix | "copper peptide serum" > "Hairgenetix serum" |

### Hairgenetix Competitive Advantage

**None of the big players dominate all three of:** microneedling + mesotherapy + copper peptides

This is the strategic opening. Hairgenetix should own the "hair regeneration systems" category:
- Not just minoxidil (Hims)
- Not just a serum (Scand Biolabs)
- Not just an ingredient (The Ordinary)
- A **complete system**: microneedling + mesotherapy + peptides
